Building a Weather App

Wireframing | High-Fidelity Mockups

The Goal:

I wanted to design a colorful weather app that focused on the most important information: What's the temperature outside and what do I need to wear to be comfortable today?

Wireframing:

I built lots of wireframes in Adobe XD to try to figure out an ideal layout for the weather app. It was easy to transition straight from the chosen wireframes to the final mockups because it was all in the same software.
